INTO THE GARDEN

Verses for Hearts Young & Old

Into The Garden is a collection of 34 original poems and illustrations by Melanie M Zwegers, inspired by childhood, nature, and the garden of verses that started it all: A Child's Garden of Verses by Robert Louis Stevenson.

Discover hidden fairy doors, fireflies at twilight, adventures on high seas, and the delights of the changing seasons inside this heartfelt collection.

Into the Garden is a book for all ages, to be enjoyed by children, parents, and grandparents alike. Enter into a place of wonder, beauty, and playful imagination, and rekindle the joys and pleasures of youth!
